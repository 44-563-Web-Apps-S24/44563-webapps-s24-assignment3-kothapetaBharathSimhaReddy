Bob Khaki the Koala

Meet Bob Khaki, a friendly koala with a soft blue hue. Bob is known for his calm demeanor and love for eucalyptus leaves. With his distinctive color and laid-back personality, he's easily recognizable in the vast Australian forests where he resides.


Games Bob Khaki enjoys:

  2. Eucalyptus Leaf Toss
